Covert Ops


Operation Resistance
Operation R was started by Governor Wilhelm and King Lenin. It is a covert operation staged in Timon to spy on the enemy and steal technology. Commander Fleet heads the operation in Timon and directs his agents. Some duties of an Agent of Operation R include spying, espionage, Assassinations, hacking, and Bombing. As an agent the most important rule is the code of silence. Operation R must maintain its secrecy at all times. Sharing any information about the Operation to anyone can result in being courtmarshalled and arrested. Members of operation R include Agents from Kandirk and Ivory.

Beta
There are rumors of a group working in timon called Beta. There is very little information on their group. What we do know is that they are somehow tied to the Eastland Republic.
