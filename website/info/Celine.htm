Celine Ashe
Race: Human
Class: Mage
Age: 24
Nationality: ????

 

 

Celine was locked in a prison cell for 2 years until Commander Fleet found her on a mission and decided to take her in. She cannot remember anything about her past or why she was imprisoned for so long. But she can vaguely remember a father figure that she was once very close to. Since then she has been working with the resistance operation. Celine has been very proficient with magick for as long as she can remember. During her years with the resistance operation she has become a whiz at hacking and spying. Commander Fleet had somewhat replaced the role of a father to her and she is one of his most loyal Operatives.
