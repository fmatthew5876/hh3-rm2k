Party Change and Reform Group Menu

This menu is not accessible in dungeons

Reform Group Menu
This menu is accessible from the main menu. Press Escape and chose Reform Group. Once you have entered this menu you can reorganize your party. The first character is the one that will appear on the map. To swap the positions of two characters move the cursor over the one you wish to change and press enter. Now move the cursor over the person you would like to swap them with. You can press escape at this time to cancel this operation. Pressing escape with no selected exits the menu.

Party Change Menu
The party change menu becomes available later in the game by talking to different characters. It is pretty simple to use. Just move the cursor over the character you want in your party then select which slot 1-4 you want them to be in.
